About Hunters Paradise Tuuti

Nestled in the serene landscapes of Bungoma, Kenya. Hunters Paradise Tuuti offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and natural beauty. Our establishment provides a tranquil retreat for both business and leisure travelers.

With our two distinct accommodation types - Single Room and Twin Room - we cater to the needs of all our guests, ensuring a memorable stay with personalized service.

Our dedicated staff is committed to providing exceptional hospitality, making your stay at Hunters Paradise Tuuti an unforgettable experience.

Our Accommodation

Single Bed Room

Our cozy single bed rooms are designed for solo travelers and couples seeking comfort and privacy. Each room is equipped with modern amenities and offers a peaceful retreat after a day of activities.

  • Comfortable single bed
  • En-suite bathroom
  • Private Balcony
  • Mini Fridge Stocked on order
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Work desk
  • Flat-screen TV

Twin Room

Perfect for friends or colleagues traveling together, our twin rooms feature two comfortable beds with premium bedding. The spacious layout ensures a comfortable stay for all guests.

  • Two comfortable single beds
  • Spacious en-suite bathroom
  • Private Balcony
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Work desk
  • Mini refrigerator stocked on order
